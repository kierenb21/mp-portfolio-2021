Typography.

I carefully chose fonts to make things clear on my website and to also structured and arranged the wording well, I chose the font “Bungee Shade” for my Title as it gave of a casual vibe but it also stood out. For the Navigation bar I chose the Font “Zen dots” this worked very well as it was a casual and clear font but gave off a bold and out standing effect, these fonts paired with the contrast of the Green and Black writing and header makes my website very clear and easy to navigate as everything is easily readably and catches your eye so you can navigate wherever the user wants to. I trailed “Alfa Sans 1” at the start of my website but as time went on the font looked out of place this would make the user feel bored and want to click off so changing fonts and layouts work as eye candy for the user. I also used the Bare bones Minimum technique (Jenni Lukac, 2011) to keep things simple I deleted all distractions and Just kept it to what was needed for the user to navigate to what they intended to do on the website.
